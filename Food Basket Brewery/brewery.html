Brewery Experience

Come see how we craft our unique beverages and experience the heart of Benue's first brewery

Discover Our Process

At Food Basket Brewery, we believe in transparency and education. Our guided tours take you through the entire brewing process, from selecting local ingredients to the final packaging of our products.

You'll see firsthand how we combine traditional brewing techniques with modern technology to create our unique beverages. Our knowledgeable guides will explain each step of the process and answer any questions you might have.

Tours conclude with a tasting session where you can sample our range of products and learn proper tasting techniques to fully appreciate the flavors and aromas of each beverage.
