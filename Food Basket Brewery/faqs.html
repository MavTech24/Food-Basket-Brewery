What makes Food Basket Brewery unique?
Food Basket Brewery is Benue's first craft brewery, specializing in beverages made with locally sourced ingredients. Our unique approach combines traditional brewing techniques with modern innovation, and our commitment to sustainability sets us apart. We work directly with local farmers to create authentic flavors that represent the best of our region.
Where can I buy your products?
Our products are available at select retailers throughout Benue State, including Makurdi Central Market, Gboko Premium Stores, and Otukpo Beverage Haven. We're also expanding to supermarkets in Abuja. You can always visit our brewery store for the full range of our products. Check our website for specific locations near you.
What are your brewery tour hours?
We offer brewery tours Tuesday through Friday from 10am to 6pm, Saturdays from 11am to 8pm, and Sundays from 12pm to 5pm. We're closed on Mondays. Tours typically last 45-60 minutes and include a tasting session. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend tours and groups larger than 6 people.
Are your beverages suitable for vegetarians/vegans?
Yes, all of our beverages are suitable for both vegetarians and vegans. We use only plant-based ingredients in our brewing process and do not use any animal-derived products or processing aids. Our commitment to natural, locally sourced ingredients means you can enjoy our products with confidence.
How do you practice sustainability?
Sustainability is at the core of our operations. We source over 80% of our ingredients from local farmers, reducing food miles and supporting the local economy. We've implemented water conservation systems that reduce our usage by 40%, and we recycle 100% of our wastewater. Our packaging uses recycled materials, and we're transitioning to solar energy with a goal of 100% renewable power by 2025.
Do you offer non-alcoholic options?
Yes, we offer several non-alcoholic options, including our Makurdi Malt and Sorghum Sparkle beverages. These are crafted with the same care and quality ingredients as our alcoholic beverages, providing refreshing options for designated drivers, those avoiding alcohol, or anyone looking for a delicious non-alcoholic alternative.
Can I host a private event at the brewery?
Absolutely! We offer private event hosting for groups of 20-100 people. Our space is perfect for corporate events, birthday celebrations, and other special occasions. We can arrange customized tours, tasting sessions, and food pairings. Please contact our events team at events@foodbasketbrewery.com for availability and pricing.
How fresh are your products?
We pride ourselves on freshness. Our products are brewed in small batches and delivered to retailers within days of packaging. Each bottle or can has a freshness date stamped on it. For the optimal experience, we recommend consuming our beers within 3 months of the packaging date and our malt beverages within 6 months.
