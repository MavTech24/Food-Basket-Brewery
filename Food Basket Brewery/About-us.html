About the FOOD BASKET BREWERY

Our Story

Discover the passion and craftsmanship behind Food Basket Brewery

Benue's First Craft Brewery

Founded in 2024, Food Basket Brewery began with a simple mission: to create exceptional beverages using the rich agricultural resources of Benue State. What started as a small operation with just three employees has grown into a thriving brewery known for quality and innovation.

Our founder, Dr Raymond Terfa Asmakaha CFA the Managing Director of Benue Investment and Property Company BIPC, recognized the potential of locally sourced grains and fruits to create unique brews that celebrate the flavors of our region while meeting international standards of excellence.

Today, we continue to honor our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community, producing a range of beverages that have earned recognition both locally and internationally.

Local Sourcing

We partner with local farmers to source the finest ingredients, supporting our community and reducing our carbon footprint.

Quality Craftsmanship

Our master brewers combine traditional techniques with modern innovation to create beverages of exceptional quality.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from water conservation to waste reduction and energy efficiency.

Community Focus

We believe in giving back to our community through job creation, local partnerships, and cultural initiatives.

Benue's pioneering brewery, crafting world-class beverages with locally sourced ingredients since 2018.
